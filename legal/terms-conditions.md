JOB CONTENT RULES
You should only post jobs that represent real and current job openings You should make sure that you have the right to post the opening, for example, ensure that the trademark owner and the copyright owner of the content have consented to you posting the job You should make sure that all elements of the job are described accurately, for example, clearly specify:

1. Whether the role is full-time or part-time; exempt or non-exempt; contractor or employee

2. Whether it’s for a fixed period

3. The required experience and skillset for the role

4. Whether the role is remote, i.e., work-from-home

5. The location of the job; including the city and state where the job seeker will work (if the role is not a work-from-home role)

6. The salary and any other compensation

7. Clear instructions for applying for the job

8. The application process must not require the job seeker to visit social media platforms and/or ‘like’ and/or ‘share’ as a condition of the application

9. The application must not be utilized as a marketing tool or lead generator
